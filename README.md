# 📊 Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Retail Sales Performance Dashboard** developed in **Power BI** to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, regional trends, and business growth. The dashboard follows a storytelling approach, allowing business users to move from overall performance to detailed analysis and finally to actionable business recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this project is to:

- Monitor overall business performance using KPIs.
- Analyze monthly sales and profit trends.
- Identify high and low-performing regions, categories, and customer segments.
- Understand key business drivers affecting profitability.
- Provide data-driven recommendations to improve profit margins and operational efficiency.

---

## 📄 Dashboard Pages

### **Page 1 – Retail Sales Performance Dashboard**
Executive overview of the business with key performance indicators and interactive visualizations.

**Includes:**
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Total Orders
- Average Discount
- Average Shipping Days
- Monthly Sales & Profit Trend
- Profit by Region
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Segment

---

### **Page 2 – Trend and Growth Analysis**

Focuses on business growth and profitability trends over time.

**Includes:**
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Monthly Profit Trend
- Monthly Sales Growth %
- Key Business Insights
- Interactive Filters (Year, Region, Segment)

---

### **Page 3 – Business Drivers and Profit Segmentation**

Analyzes the major factors influencing business profitability.

**Includes:**
- Regional Category Profit Matrix
- Profit by Segment
- Profit by Category
- Interactive Filters
- Comparative Profit Analysis

---

### **Page 4 – Business Recommendations and Action Plan**

Converts analytical findings into practical business recommendations.

**Includes:**
- Executive Summary
- Three Business Recommendations
- Top 5 Low-Profit Products
- Way Forward
- Action Plan

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Profit in 2024 declined compared to 2023 despite stable sales performance.
- Monthly profit shows fluctuations instead of consistent growth.
- Regional and category profitability varies significantly.
- Certain products generate lower profits and require pricing review.
- Business recommendations focus on improving profitability through better pricing, product strategy, and operational efficiency.
